The tertiary education Commission has Ceased its operations with effect from 16 january 2020.Its powers and functions have passed to the two new statutory bodies.The Higher Education Commission (HEC) ad the Quality Assurance Authority (QAA).The Higher Education Commission (HEC) is the Regulatory Body for tertiary education.until Further Notice, both the statutory bodies are sharing the same physical address as the former TEC.

Moniter and oversee the higher education system;

The availability of adequate human resources, physical infrastructure and other material resources;
